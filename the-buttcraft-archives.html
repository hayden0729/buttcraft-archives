Full Timeline

This is only Buttcraft's timeline. Things that happened IRL or in other servers/calls are mostly not mentioned.

  • August 6, 2018: The server is started by Henry and named by Will. Jonathan and Hayden join. The music bot of choice is Rythm.
  • August 8, 2018: Nick B joins the Minecraft server, so he's added to the Discord.
  • August 17, 2018: Tyler joins as well.
  • August 22, 2018: A user called "ADSDDDDDDDDD" joins the server. I have no idea who this could've possibly been. The account was created that day.
  • August 31, 2018: Michael's added, too.
  • September 4, 2018: The 2018-2019 school year starts, and Minecraft is forgotten for a while (mostly in favor of League of Legends...)
  • January 29, 2019: Simon joins for a Minecraft server that Will's trying to get started, but it never goes anywhere.
  • August 18, 2019: A year after Buttcraft I, Buttcraft II is started, and the server's brought back.
  • February 1, 2020: After another phase of dormancy, the server's revived again after Nick B buys a realm and Buttcraft III is started. Aidan joins for the new realm.
  • February 8, 2020: Zephan joins, too.
  • April 10, 2020: One last hiatus comes to an end as Buttcraft IV is started shortly after the start of quarantine. From now on, Buttcraft is the main Discord server.
  • April 16, 2020: Alongside Buttcraft IV, the first TTT phase begins.
  • April 23, 2020: In a Minecraft trial, Tyler is found guilty of animal cruelty, damage to property, and several murders. Thomas joins the server for the event. After the trial, a Dune 2000 match is held that kicks off an OpenRA phase (which occurs in tandem with a CS:GO phase and the Buttcraft IV phase).
  • May 9, 2020: Henry is declared the winner of the Red Alert tournament, with Michael as the runner-up. At this point, the CS:GO phase has ended, but Buttcraft IV is still going strong.
  • May 17, 2020: Father of Asahd has its first birthday. Additionally, Kyle joins the server for a large Red Alert game. Buttcraft IV has ended, but a singleplayer phase begins to start up (origin of Hugo F. Oglert).
  • June 3, 2020: The singleplayer and Red Alert phases have died down a bit. Sleep schedules are worsening. The Stardew Valley phase begins.
  • June 25, 2020: In the midst of Stardew Valley and another singleplayer phase, Bobby's added for Red Alert.
  • July 3, 2020: For the first time in all of quarantine, League of Legends rears its ugly head. It becomes a small part of the regular routine, but it's not super prominent yet.
  • July 9, 2020: Buttcraft V is started, bringing a surge of activity to the server. It keeps League under control.
  • July 19, 2020: Marshall joins, probably for League.
  • July 21, 2020: Buttcraft V dies down, and League takes over. It rules the server for a while.
  • August 12, 2020: A TFT phase (on part 2 of the Galaxies set) and a Civ 6 phase come out of nowhere.
  • August 21, 2020: TTT joins the routine. There are more people than there were in the first TTT phase, which makes things a lot more exciting.
  • August 29, 2020: Alongside TFT, TTT, and League (which has snuck its way back into the server), a Star Wars movie-watching phase starts right before the beginning of the school year.
  • September 8, 2020: The 2020-2021 school year starts on Microsoft Teams. This day goes on to be the one with the most messages (912) in Buttcraft's history.
  • September 17, 2020: Alongside school, League, and a little TFT (set 4 now), the Oregon Trail Minecraft server is started.
  • September 19, 2020: Everyone gets Among Us and Kyle's server is invaded. It'll be gone within a month.
  • September 24, 2020: Oregon is reached. Town & railroad construction begins, and lasts for a week or two before the server fades away.
  • October 4, 2020: After a few weeks of Minecraft, presidential debates, TFT, League, and Among Us, Armored Patrol takes over for a little bit. Kyle leaves the server.
  • October 8, 2020: Nick V joins the server.
  • October 9, 2020: Once Armored Patrol gets old, a little singleplayer phase begins.
  • October 13, 2020: You've heard of the troposphere, stratosphere, mesosphere, thermosphere, and exosphere, but what about the buenosphere?
  • October 20, 2020: Buttcraft V returns. This time, the phase lasts for over a month, and it becomes the most developed Minecraft server to date.
  • November 9, 2020: Nick joins the wrong Google Hangout.
  • November 17, 2020: New routine: play Minecraft and League by day, and watch Evangelion by night.
  • November 25, 2020: Thanksgiving break starts. Buttcraft V is out, and the Westbound phase begins. It's followed by Blox Cards, then Ace of Spades.
  • December 13, 2020: A new singleplayer phase starts.
  • December 23, 2020: Winter break starts, and it's back to TTT at night. The Christmas song tier list is made. Big Chungus Candyland goes down on New Year's. The singleplayer phase continues during the day.
  • January 2, 2021: Winter break comes to a close, and the Survivor: Cagayan phase starts at night.
  • January 9, 2021: Rythm is declared irrelevant. Groovy is the new music bot king.
  • January 13, 2021: A new Red Alert era is started. Olivia Rodrigo begins to take the server by storm.
  • January 26, 2021: The first part of songvid 2 is uploaded. At this point, it's still fun to make.
  • February 4, 2021: A return to League is made after staying away from it for a while. Some people who haven't yet been exposed to the drug find themselves trying it out. Some won't recover.
  • February 17, 2021: A brief final revival of Buttcraft V is started on a snow day. This coincides with the start of a FNAF lore phase, which will soon be followed by one of actually playing the games.
  • March 24, 2021: After a month of playing League and watching up on the FNAF lore during school lunch, the games are explored. Henry and Aidan play the first one, followed by Hayden on the second one, then Simon on the third. After that, though, the phase dies out, and it's back to the Summoner's Rift. We play some little one-off games, but the prominent activity is all League.
  • April 3, 2021: Michael plays all of Hamilton once, and Hamilton Friday is conceived. It's not serious yet, but by the end of the year, this server will know nearly every line.
  • May 21, 2021: Olivia Rodrigo drops SOUR. Buttcraft is never the same.
  • July 14, 2021: The SevTech: Ages server is started. Nobody has any idea what horrors the Betweenlands dimension has in store.
  • August 8, 2021: After a brief hiatus for various members' vacations, Buttcraft is back in action for League of Legends.
  • August 28, 2021: A small Terraria phase goes down in between all the League.
  • August 30, 2021: Groovy is struck down, leading to a hectic month in which there are no reliable music bots. Eventually, Nifty's hacked together to solve this issue.
  • September 4, 2021: Sven Co-Op is discovered, and has its own miniature phase. Eventually, it'll come back, mostly for the Capture the Flag map.
  • September 30, 2021: The amplified Minecraft server is started. We don't get too far, but it gets a bit of play.
  • December 4, 2021: After a chill period without much League (mostly micro-phases, like Roller Coaster Tycoon, Bananagrams, and Ace of Spades), another Minecraft server is started, this time for the 1.18 update. It also doesn't get too far, but it's fun while it lasts.
  • December 7, 2021: Donovan's indoctrinated into the League cult. It's not long before he finds himself addicted, too.
  • December 31, 2022: A new TFT phase is started with the latest set.
  • January 2, 2022: Additionally, a miniature Red Alert/Civilization phase is started with the newcomer, Donovan.
  • January 13, 2022: The RA/Civ phase fades out in favor of a brief singleplayer phase followed by a return to League after its short break.
  • February 18, 2022: A new Minecraft server is started, and people stick around this one longer than the last one. Paul joins Buttcraft for it, but he doesn't play.
  • February 25, 2022: Collin joins the server. He quickly adapts to the username convention.
  • March 6, 2022: The first 10-man League matches are held. Two games are played, and in both, Bobby, Zephan, Nick B, Marshall, and Henry are beaten by Aidan, Hayden, Michael, Donovan, and Jonathan.
  • March 13, 2022: 5AM League games become a tradition for a minute.
  • March 25, 2022: A short non-League phase starts, featuring Sven Co-op CTF and TTT.
  • May 14, 2022: After a long period of League, a brief Minecraft server is started with a shared base, similar to the format used in Buttcraft IV. Sven Co-op CTF also makes a return for a bit.
  • May 27, 2022: songvid 2 is finally over.
  • June 9, 2022: Another Minecraft server is started for the 1.19 update. The gimmick this time is that it's impossible to read the coordinates, as they each have five digits.
  • June 19, 2022: A Civilization 6 phase happens.
  • July 12, 2022: A storm passes through Havre de Grace and most people lose power for several days. Also, Paul disappears from the server.
  • July 17, 2022: A CSGO/Stardew Valley phase starts up. CSGO goes on to the present day.
  • August 6, 2022: With the exception of Donovan, everyone's graduated, so Buttcraft (started on this day four years ago, right before the start of our freshman year) is graduating, too. As it moves into a new era, this page is created to preserve the last one.
